Tips & Advice

view all
  • 01

    Check tyre pressure regularly (every 2-4 weeks) for safer drive and to make them last longer.

  • 02

    If tread wear bars of hard rubber are visible its time to change your tyre. These are specially designed to be visible when your tyre’s tread has become worn. (mark on the sidewall will help you locate the same)

  • 03

    If you see crazing i.e. small cracks in the side wall you should replace your tyres. Sidewalls aren’t very thick and a damaged sidewall can cause your tyre to fail
